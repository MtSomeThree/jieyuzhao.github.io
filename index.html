About me

I am now a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science, UCLA. I work with Prof. Kai-Wei Chang focusing on problems in Natural Language Processing and machine learning. Currently, I am working on detecting gender bias in modern machine learning systems. My resume is available here.

Before joining UCLA, I spent one year in Department of Computer Science, University of Virginia. Had a great experience in Charlottesville!
